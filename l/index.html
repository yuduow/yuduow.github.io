Hi! I'm Yuduo Wu (吴雨多).

I'm a Deep Learning Engineer at an Artificial Intelligence startup NovuMind Inc. Before, I was a Software Engineer at IBM Watson and Cloud Platform team worked on IBM® Bluemix and SoftLayer®. I obtained my Master's degree and worked as Graduate Student Researcher in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Davis, focused on GPU computing and graph analytics. I also did my summer research internship at DARPA (Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency). I was very fortunate to be advised by Professor John D. Owens. My thesis can be found here.
